Isocratean Logoi

Moving away from Aristotle’s forensic discipline, Isocrates suggests logos calls community into being. Peitho here is the energy of assembly.

Honoring Complexity: Isocrates’ "dwelling" was elite and exclusionary. We must avoid a progressive narrative that ignores his pan-Hellenic elitism.

Ethos as Dwelling

Ethos is not a tool for credibility, but a dwelling place (Hyde). Peitho becomes the atmosphere of this shared site.

Foss & Griffin’s recovery of the listener's agency. A return to the 'feminine' instability of Peitho where outcomes are emergent.
